## Customer Segments
- **Non‑technical family members** (parents, grandparents, adult children) who need a simple way to access and manage self‑hosted services.  
- **Small family‑run businesses** (home‑based e‑commerce, tutoring, crafts) that want a low‑maintenance internal portal.  
- **Senior citizens** seeking a secure, click‑to‑run home server dashboard.  
- **DIY home‑automation enthusiasts** who run Raspberry Pi or NAS devices but lack deep sysadmin skills.  
- **Tech‑savvy relatives** who act as “family IT admins” and will purchase on behalf of the household.

## Value Propositions
- **One‑click deployment** (Docker Compose / Helm) – no command‑line required.  
- **Intuitive UI** tailored for lay‑users: drag‑and‑drop service tiles, natural‑language help, multilingual (English/Thai).  
- **Built‑in security hardening** (auto TLS, role‑based access, audit logs) to protect family data.  
- **Unified portal** to launch, stop, and monitor common self‑hosted apps (file sharing, media streaming, calendars).  
- **Low‑maintenance** auto‑updates and health‑checks, reducing the need for ongoing technical support.

## Channels
- **GitHub repository** with detailed README, issue tracker, and release binaries.  
- **Docker Hub** image for instant pull‑and‑run.  
- **Community forums & Discord** for peer support and feature requests.  
- **Partnerships with home‑router/NAS vendors** (e.g., ASUS, Synology) to bundle kin‑portal as an optional app store entry.  
- **Tutorial video series** on YouTube and localized Thai subtitles (Thai market focus).  

## Customer Relationships
- **Self‑service knowledge base** (FAQs, step‑by‑step guides, video walkthroughs).  
- **Community‑driven support** via Discord and GitHub Discussions.  
- **Premium support plans** (email/phone SLA, remote troubleshooting).  
- **Onboarding webinars** for families purchasing the managed‑hosting tier.  
- **Feedback loops** – regular surveys and beta‑program invitations to co‑create new features.
