# Marketing Plan

## Positioning
**Primary:** "Kin-Portal: Simplify self-hosted service management for non-technical family members."
**Alternatives:**
1. "Kin-Portal: Your family's gateway to seamless self-hosted service management."
2. "Kin-Portal: Empowering non-tech family members to manage self-hosted services with ease."
3. "Kin-Portal: The user-friendly platform for family-friendly self-hosted service management."

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
1. **Persona:** Sarah Johnson
   - **Daily Job:** Managing family schedules, handling household tasks, and coordinating with family members.
   - **Budget:** $50-$100/month for tools that simplify her life.
2. **Persona:** David Lee
   - **Daily Job:** Ensuring family members can access necessary services without technical hassles.
   - **Budget:** $75-$150/month for solutions that reduce his workload.
3. **Persona:** Emily Rodriguez
   - **Daily Job:** Coordinating family activities and ensuring everyone has access to required services.
   - **Budget:** $100-$200/month for tools that streamline family management.

## Channels
1. **Channel:** Social Media Advertising (Facebook, Instagram)
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30-$50 per acquisition
2. **Channel:** Influencer Partnerships (Family Tech Bloggers)
   - **CAC Estimate:** $40-$60 per acquisition
3. **Channel:** SEO & Content Marketing (Blog Posts, Tutorials)
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20-$40 per acquisition

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Introduction to self-hosted services and common challenges.
- **Week 5-8:** Benefits of Kin-Portal and how it solves family management issues.
- **Week 9-12:** Customer testimonials, case studies, and success stories.
- **Week 13-16:** Advanced features, tips, and tricks for maximizing Kin-Portal usage.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize marketing materials, set up social media accounts, and prepare influencer partnerships.
- **D-0:** Launch Kin-Portal with a promotional campaign across all channels.
- **D+30:** Gather initial user feedback, optimize marketing strategies, and address any issues.
- **D+90:** Evaluate success metrics, scale successful campaigns, and plan for long-term growth.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Aim for 1,000 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** Achieve $10,000 MRR by D+90.
