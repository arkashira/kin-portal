 # Partner Targets

This document outlines the partner integration roadmap for the `kin-portal` product, a user-friendly, self-hosted service platform that simplifies access and management for non-technical family members. The goal is to integrate with SaaS/APIs that will enhance the user experience, solve specific jobs, and potentially generate revenue through affiliate/revenue-share programs.

## Partner Integrations (Priority: High to Low)

### 1. **Google Drive (Integration Effort: Medium, Value-Add: Storage & Backup Solution)**

- Integration: Allow users to easily backup and restore their kin-portal data on Google Drive.
- Free-Tier Limit: 15GB of storage for free accounts.
- Rationale: Google Drive is a widely-used cloud storage service that can provide a seamless backup and restore solution for kin-portal users.

### 2. **Twilio (Integration Effort: Medium, Value-Add: Two-Factor Authentication)**

- Integration: Implement Twilio's two-factor authentication (2FA) to enhance the security of user accounts.
- Free-Tier Limit: SMS messages are free up to 1,000 messages per month.
- Rationale: Twilio's 2FA service can help protect user accounts from unauthorized access, providing an additional layer of security.

### 3. **Stripe (Integration Effort: Medium, Value-Add: Payment Processing)**

- Integration: Incorporate Stripe's payment processing service to allow users to easily purchase premium features or services.
- Free-Tier Limit: None for basic payment processing.
- Rationale: Stripe is a popular and widely-used payment processing service that can provide a seamless payment experience for kin-portal users.

### 4. **Slack (Integration Effort: Medium, Value-Add: Notifications & Collaboration)**

- Integration: Integrate Slack for real-time notifications and collaboration among family members.
- Free-Tier Limit: 10,000 messages per day, 5GB of file storage, and 10 integrations.
- Rationale: Slack can help improve communication and collaboration among family members, making it easier for them to stay connected and manage their kin-portal accounts.

### 5. **Zapier (Integration Effort: Low, Value-Add: Automation & Integration with Other Services)**

- Integration: Implement Zapier to automate tasks and integrate kin-portal with other popular services.
- Free-Tier Limit: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks per month, and 2-step Zaps.
- Rationale: Zapier can help automate repetitive tasks and integrate kin-portal with other services, enhancing the user experience and saving time.

### 6. **AWS Amplify (Integration Effort: High, Value-Add: Mobile App Development)**

- Integration: Use AWS Amplify to develop a mobile app for kin-portal, providing users with a convenient way to access and manage their accounts on-the-go.
- Free-Tier Limit: 1 million requests per month for most services.
- Rationale: A mobile app can provide a more convenient and user-friendly experience for kin-portal users, especially those who are not technically savvy.

### 7. **Mailchimp (Integration Effort: Low, Value-Add: Email Marketing)**

- Integration: Integrate Mailchimp for email marketing campaigns to promote kin-portal and engage users.
- Free-Tier Limit: 12,000 emails per month, 2,000 subscribers, and basic automation.
- Rationale: Email marketing can help promote kin-portal to a wider audience and engage existing users, potentially driving growth and revenue.

### 8. **Calendly (Integration Effort: Low, Value-Add: Scheduling Appointments)**

- Integration: Integrate Calendly for scheduling appointments with family members or customer support.
- Free-Tier Limit: 10 meetings per month, 1 calendar, and basic integrations.
- Rationale: Calendly can help streamline the scheduling process, making it easier for family members to connect and for customer support to manage their appointments.
